Carriers

Shipstore works with multiple carriers for our clients to be able to process shipments, receive rates, generate labels, and track their shipments. Carriers can be either small parcel or LTL.

Small Parcel

  • DHL
  • FedEx
    • SpeeDee
  • UPS
  • USPS

LTL

  • Estes
  • FedEx Freight
  • Old Dominion Freight Line
  • Saia
