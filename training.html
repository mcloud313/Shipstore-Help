Training Clients

We have two options for training sessions either clients that request it or new clients.

  1. We use TeamViewer to get remote access to do the training on one of the clients stations and everyone can watch that screen. This is the best route if working with an integration.
  2. We can send a Teams meeting invite to do the training from your station and everyone can remote in via Teams. This route is preferred when there is a multitude of staff to train and we don't need access to the station for loading/saving operations.
