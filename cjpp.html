CJ Pony Parts

CJ Pony parts is is a retailer that sells automotive restoration and performance products. CJPP sells quality aftermarket parts and accessories for Ford Mustangs, Broncos, Focus STs, F-100 classic trucks, and the Chevy C10. CJPP is an impact private level customer. They host their own server on location and have two main locations of operations Mechanicsburg, Pennsylvania, and Las Vegas, Nevada. They have two process flows a manual shipping process and a conveyor line. The conveyor line works by sending a small bit of data to the system and the system pulls order and ships and sends data back. This is what CJPP means when they say "sent it down the line".

SSO Authorization

CJPP is moving towards SSO authorization, which means they'll use Microsoft's Active Directory for authorization with Shipstore and the merchant app.
